Architecture of Santa Maria del Fiore

```

Brunelleschi's Dome

The dome of Santa Maria del Fiore was designed by Filippo Brunelleschi. It is considered one of the greatest achievements of Renaissance architecture.

Giotto's Bell Tower

The bell tower was designed by Giotto and is one of the most famous examples of Gothic architecture in Italy.

The Façade

The cathedral façade is decorated with white, green, and pink marble, creating a distinctive appearance.
