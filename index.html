# Santa Maria del Fiore Welcome to our digital humanities project. ## History Santa Maria del Fiore, also known as the Florence Cathedral, is one of the most famous landmarks in Italy. Its construction began in 1296 under the direction of the architect Arnolfo di Cambio. The cathedral was built to replace the older Church of Santa Reparata and to demonstrate the wealth and importance of Florence during the Middle Ages. The most remarkable feature of the cathedral is its enormous dome, designed by Filippo Brunelleschi. Construction of the dome started in 1420 and was completed in 1436. At that time, it was considered an extraordinary engineering achievement because it was the largest dome ever built without traditional wooden support structures. The cathedral’s exterior is decorated with white, green, and pink marble, creating a beautiful and unique appearance.
